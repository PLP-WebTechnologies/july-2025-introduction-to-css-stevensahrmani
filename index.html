About Us

Who we area?

EdTech Hub Academy (ETHA) is a pioneering tech school dedicated to empowering the next generation of innovators, enterprenuers, and leaders.

Our team comprises experienced educators, industry experts, and passionate individuals who share a common goal: to bridge the gap betweeneducation and industry needs.

What we do?

At EdTech Hub Academy, we design and deliver cutting-edge programs, training, and resources to equip students with the skills, knowledge, and mindset required to thrive in the digital economy. Our focus areas include:

  • Technology education and training:

    • This area focuses on equipping educators and learners with the necessary skills to effectively use technology in educational settings.

  • Digital literacy and skills development:

    • This area aims to enhance the digital skills of both educators and students, ensuring they can navigate and utilize technology effectively.

  • Enterpreneurships and innovation:

    • This area fosters an entrepreneurial mindset and encourages innovation in educational practices and solutions.

  • Industry partnerships and collaboration:

    • This area focuses on building strong partnerships between educational institutions and industry stakeholders to enhance the relevance and impact of education.

  • Equity in Education:

    • This area addresses issues of equity and access in education, ensuring all learners have the opportunity to succeed.

Our Approach

At EdTech Hub Academy, we believe in a holistic approach to education that combines technology, pedagogy, and real-world application. Our programs are designed to be flexible, inclusive, and responsive to the needs of our learners.

We leverage the latest advancements in educational technology to create engaging and interactive learning experiences. Our team is committed to continuous improvement and innovation, ensuring that our programs remain relevant and effective.

Our Courses

Course 1: Introduction to EdTech

This course covers the basics of education technology and its applications.

It also provides an overview of educational technology and its impact on teaching and learning.

Learn the basics of educational technology and how it can transform learning experiences.

Course 2: EdTech Tools and Resources

This course explores various educational technology tools and resources available for educators and learners.

It covers topics such as learning management systems, online collaboration tools, and digital content creation.

Discover how to effectively integrate these tools into your teaching practice.

Course 3: Google Certified Educator Level 1

This course prepares educators to become Google Certified Educators at Level 1.

It covers the core skills and knowledge needed to effectively use Google Workspace for Education tools in the classroom.

Participants will learn how to integrate Google tools into their teaching practice to enhance student learning.

Course 4: Google Certified Educator Level 2

This course is designed for educators who have already achieved Google Certified Educator Level 1.

It delves deeper into advanced features and functionalities of Google Workspace for Education tools.

Participants will learn how to leverage these tools to create innovative and engaging learning experiences.

Course 5: AWS Educate Cloud Computing Course

This course provides an introduction to cloud computing concepts and AWS services.

It focuses on developing training skills and knowledge of AWS tools and services.

Participants will learn how to design and deliver effective professional development sessions for educators.

Course 6: STEAM Education Course

This course focuses on integrating science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics (STEAM) into the classroom.

Participants will explore innovative teaching strategies and hands-on activities to engage students in STEAM learning.

Learn how to create a dynamic and inclusive STEAM curriculum that fosters creativity and critical thinking.

Course 7: Digital Citizenship and Online Safety

This course covers the importance of digital citizenship and online safety for educators and students.

Participants will learn about responsible online behavior, digital footprints, and strategies for staying safe online.

Discover how to teach students about the ethical use of technology and the importance of protecting their personal information.

Course 8: Inclusive Education and Special Needs

This course focuses on creating inclusive learning environments for students with special needs.

Participants will learn about different types of disabilities, strategies for differentiation, and assistive technologies.

Explore how to adapt teaching methods and materials to meet the diverse needs of all learners.

Course 9: Data-Driven Decision Making in Education

This course explores the use of data to inform educational practices and improve student outcomes.

Participants will learn how to collect, analyze, and interpret data to make informed decisions in the classroom.

Discover how to use data to identify areas for improvement and track student progress effectively.

Course 10: Digital Marketing and AI-Powered Tools Course

This course provides an overview of digital marketing strategies and the use of AI-powered tools.

Participants will learn how to create effective digital marketing campaigns and leverage AI for data analysis.

Explore the ethical considerations and best practices for using AI in marketing.
